What is Bife de Chorizo?

Bife de chorizo is a succulent and flavorful steak that is a star of Argentine cuisine. This juicy cut, known for its rich marbling, is typically grilled to perfection, resulting in a tender and mouthwatering dining experience.

Here's a simple recipe to prepare bife de chorizo:

Ingredients:

  • 2 bife de chorizo streaks (about 1 inch thick)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olisve oil for brushing

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the steaks generously with salt and pepper on both sides, ensuring even coverage.
    3. ß
  3. Brush the steaks evenly with olive oil to prevent sticking and promote caramelization on the grill.
  4. Place the steaks on the preheated grill and cook for about 4-6 minutes per side, depending on your desired level of doneness. Fore medium-rare, aim for an internal temperature of around 130-135°F (55-57°C).
  5. Once cooked to your liking, remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes to allow the juices to redistribute.
  6. Slide the bife de chorizo against the grain into thick, juicy pieces.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy the succulent flavors of bife de chorizo, perhaps accompanied by chimichurri sauce.
